Our Mission

BetterJoy was created with a simple yet ambitious mission: to provide gamers with the best possible experience when using Nintendo Switch controllers on their Windows PC. We believe that exceptional hardware deserves exceptional software support.

Our goal is to eliminate the barriers between gaming platforms, allowing you to use your favorite controllers across different ecosystems without compromise. Whether you're gaming on emulators or native PC games, BetterJoy ensures your controllers work flawlessly with full motion control support.

The Story Behind BetterJoy

BetterJoy emerged from a genuine need in the gaming community. As emulation and PC gaming grew in popularity, users found themselves frustrated with limited controller support and missing features like motion controls. The Nintendo Switch controllers, with their advanced gyroscope and accelerometer capabilities, had tremendous potential that wasn't being fully utilized on PC.

What started as a personal project quickly gained traction as gamers discovered the power of having full motion control support for their favorite emulators and games. The community response was overwhelming, with users contributing feedback, bug reports, and feature requests that helped shape BetterJoy into the robust solution it is today.

Today, BetterJoy is used by thousands of gamers worldwide, from casual players enjoying nostalgic titles to competitive gamers who demand precision and low latency. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement ensures that BetterJoy remains the premier choice for Nintendo Switch controller support on Windows.
