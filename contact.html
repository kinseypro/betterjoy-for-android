Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with BetterJoy?

Download the latest version from our download page, extract the files, and run the executable. Make sure your controllers are paired via Bluetooth first.

What controllers are supported?

BetterJoy supports all Nintendo Switch controllers including Joy-Cons (left, right, or paired), Pro Controllers, and third-party controllers that follow Nintendo's protocol.

Does it work with emulators?

Yes! BetterJoy works excellently with popular emulators like Cemu, Yuzu, Dolphin, and more, providing full motion control support.

Is BetterJoy free?

Absolutely! BetterJoy is completely free and open-source software. You can use it, modify it, and contribute to its development.
